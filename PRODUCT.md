# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Citizens requiring government services (IDs, schemes, complaints) who value speed, clarity, and trust.

## Product Purpose
To make interacting with government services feel effortless, modern, and transparent. Bridging the gap between the citizen and the state through high-quality digital experiences.

## Brand Personality
Authoritative, Precise, Effortless

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS "slop" (e.g., purple/neon gradients, identical 3-column cards, floating 3D emojis, centered generic text blocks, cartoon illustrations).

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Cleverness:** Interface choices must serve the user's intent to find information or complete forms.
- **Hardware-Accelerated Trust:** High-end motion physics and crisp aesthetics communicate a high-functioning state apparatus.
- **Asymmetry is Agency:** Avoid centered, perfectly balanced blocks in favor of dynamic, weighted layouts that feel active.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. High contrast typography required (>= 4.5:1). Support for reduced motion preferences.
